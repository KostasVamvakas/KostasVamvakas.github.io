HI, I'M KONSTANTINOS VAMVAKAS

I'm an Electrical and Computer Engineer with a Passion for Low Level Software and Hardware Design

I am currently studying Electrical and Computer Engineering at NTUA, on track to graduate with honors.

I am a member of Oceanos and a junior engineer in the Circuits & Systems Group, combining hardware and software design.

As an ECE student I have a strong interest in hardware–software co-design, embedded systems, and computer architectures. My work focuses on designing reliable electronic systems and exploring how circuit-level behavior can be leveraged for computation.

As a member of Oceanos, the electric racing boat team, I have designed multiple PCBs for onboard systems deployed on the racing vessel, with an emphasis on robustness, careful component selection, and real-world operating conditions. In parallel, I am a junior engineer with the Circuits & Systems Group at NTUA, where I am currently working on electronic oscillators and Ising machines, gaining exposure to nonlinear dynamics and oscillator-based computing.

Through these experiences, I combine practical engineering on deployed systems with research-oriented circuit and system design, bridging theory, experimentation, and real-world constraints.
