12V DC–DC Converter

Designed in KiCad for Oceanos — power conversion for onboard subsystems.

← Back to PCB list

Converts the onboard supply to a stable 12V rail for vehicle subsystems.

Role
Component selection + Schematic capture + PCB layout
Tools
KiCad · DFM-minded layout
Context
Several subsystems required a stable 12 V supply derived from the main power bus to drive sensors, controllers, and auxiliary electronics. A dedicated converter was needed to ensure clean power delivery while tolerating electrical noise and load transients.
Outcome
The 12 V converter provided stable and reliable power across operating conditions, with no observed power-related issues during integration and testing. Its clean output improved the robustness of downstream electronics and simplified system-level power distribution.

Design highlights

  • Dedicated 12 V DC-DC conversion stage.
  • Layout optimized for low ripple and transient response.
  • Designed for reliable operation in a mixed-signal, power-dense environment.
