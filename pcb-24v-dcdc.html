24V DC–DC Converter

Designed in KiCad for Oceanos — dedicated 24V rail for higher-voltage loads.

← Back to PCB list

Provides a stable 24V output for onboard subsystems.

Role
Component selection + Schematic capture + PCB layout
Tools
KiCad · DFM-minded layout
Context
The system required a reliable 24 V rail to power distributed electronics operating in a noisy, high-power environment.
Outcome
The converter delivered stable, low-noise 24 V power across operating conditions, with no observed power-related failures during testing.

Design highlights

  • Dedicated 24 V DC-DC topology with efficiency and thermal performance in mind.
  • Integrated EMI input/output filtering.
  • Careful component selection and layout for low ripple and noise.
  • Designed for reliability in electrically harsh environments.
