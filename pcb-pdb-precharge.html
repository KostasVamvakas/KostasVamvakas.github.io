PDB — Motor Precharge

Supports the motor precharge path and related protection/control circuitry.

← Back to PCB list

Designed to support the precharge sequence hardware and integration, with emphasis on layout robustness and connector accessibility.

Role
Schematic capture + PCB layout
Tools
KiCad · DFM-minded layout
Context
OTo safely energize the motor drive system, a controlled precharge stage was required to limit inrush current and protect sensitive power electronics.
Outcome
The precharge circuit ensured safe and repeatable system startup, reducing electrical stress on components and improving system reliability.

Design highlights

  • Controlled precharge sequence for high-voltage systems.
  • Protection components for fault and transient handling.
  • Clear separation of power and control paths.
